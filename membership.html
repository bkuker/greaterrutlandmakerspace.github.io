How To Join

How To Join

  1. Come to the Greater Rutland Makerspace for a New Member Orientation, shown on our calendar.
  2. Get a tour of the space, and choose your type of membership.
  3. Fill out some paperwork.
  4. Set up your payments.
  5. Start making amazing things!

Once you are a member you can use the Makerspace even if there is not a staff member or volunteer on duty, but we need to meet you on person to help you join. If there is not a convenient time on the calendar email us, or message us on Facebook, and we'll work it out!
