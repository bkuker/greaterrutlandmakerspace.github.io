--- layout: post title: VELCO Party to benefit the Makerspace ---

VELCO is turning 60 and we want you to help us celebrate.

For 60 years, Rutland-based VELCO has served Vermont through collaboration and innovation. To celebrate this milestone, we are having a party to honor our past and support the next generation of innovators.

On Thursday, October 27, 5:30-7:30, join us at the Vermont Farmers Food Center in downtown Rutland for a fun and energizing event - local musicians, local food, and a showcase of local makers' magic. A short presentation to celebrate VELCO's past, present and the future as a part of the Rutland and Vermont community will cap the evening.

Tickets are $20 per person. All proceeds from the event support the creation of the Greater Rutland Makerspace, an initiative supported by Rutland Economic Development Corporation (REDC).
